Indie

Indie Graphics are graphics that sometimes take a more articulated approach to their graphics. They have unique styles or reference older video game graphic styles.

Stardew Valley uses a unique camera angle and pixel art style to give the game a feel similar to older 16-bit games.

Night in the Woods uses a simple cartoon style to create the characters and background, giving the game a unique charm.

Journey also uses a unique art style in their graphics, with an amazing use of color to make the world come alive.

Many gaming systems feature indie-style games. Steam, Nintendo Switch, and Play Station 5 all have games with unique art styles.
