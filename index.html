Video games have existed for nearly seventy years and have grown and evolved since their initial creation. From the 1950s to today, let’s journey through their history.

The Gallery provides videos and pictures from video games in each era of video game graphics. From 8-bit to Realism, journey through the different visuals and learn about the evolution of video games.

The Timeline provides a chronological look at how video games got to where they are today. Starting with the first video game made on an analog computer and ending in present-day consoles, the timeline details hardware and software advancements that brought players video games throughout the years.

The Interactives page provides playable video games with different graphic types. You can experience 8-bit, 16-bit, and 3D games that are similar to other widely known games.
