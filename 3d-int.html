3D Interactive

This example 3D game is a Taxi Simulator, similar to the arcade game Crazy Taxi. The goal is to pick up passengers and drop them off at their destination. The red circle on the map indicates where the passenger is, and the green circle indicates their destination. Use WASD or the arrow keys to steer, Space to hand break, and C to change the camera.
